Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Introduction

StackDevOps8999 ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Military Federal Retirement Planner mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Military Federal Retirement Planner is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We want to be clear:

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT transmit your data to our servers
  • We do NOT share your information with third parties
  • We do NOT use analytics or advertising services
  • We do NOT require you to create an account

How Your Data is Stored

All retirement calculations, profile information, and financial data you enter into the App are stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Personal profile information (name, dates, service details)
  • Salary and compensation data
  • Retirement calculation inputs and results
  • Family member profiles
  • Any notes or saved calculations

Your data remains on your device and is never uploaded to external servers or cloud storage unless you explicitly choose to back up your device through your operating system's backup features (iCloud, Google Drive, etc.).

Backend Server Communication

The App connects to our backend server hosted on Railway.app solely to retrieve:

  • Current federal and military pay tables
  • VA disability compensation rates
  • TRICARE premium rates
  • Thrift Savings Plan formulas
  • Social Security benefit formulas
  • Federal and State tax rates

These requests do NOT include any of your personal information or calculation data. The server only provides reference data needed for accurate calculations.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on:

  • Your device's security features (passcode, biometric authentication)
  • Your device's operating system security
  • Physical security of your device

We recommend enabling device encryption and security features provided by your phone's operating system.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual profiles within the App
  • Clear all data using the App's reset function
  • Uninstall the App to remove all data from your device

Since we don't store your data on our servers, there is no data for us to delete on our end.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 17. We do not store information nor do we knowingly collect information from children. The App is designed for adults planning their retirement benefits.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and on this page. The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external connection is to our backend server for retrieving public pay table data, tax rates, and estimated retirement planning formulas.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal information on our servers, traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion requests) don't apply in the usual sense. You have direct control over all your data through the App interface on your device.

California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have certain rights regarding their personal information. However, since we do not collect, store, or share your personal information, there is no data for us to provide, delete, or manage.

International Users

The App is designed primarily for U.S. military personnel, veterans, and federal employees. However, users from any location can use the App. Regardless of your location, we do not collect or transmit your personal data.
